Introducing Andromeda

Andromeda is a cloud-agnostic cybersecurity and compliance automation platform developed by Bitfastt Inc., the affiliate subsidiary of LNB Federal Corporation. Purpose-built for U.S. federal agencies and defense contractors, Andromeda simplifies the Risk Management Framework (RMF) by automating control validation, evidence collection, and continuous monitoring across FISMA, NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5, FedRAMP, DFARS, and CMMC 2.0.

Supporting Microsoft Azure Government, AWS GovCloud, and future IL4/IL5 on-prem workloads, Andromeda integrates Infrastructure as Code (IaC), Compliance as Code, and role-based access control to enforce Zero Trust architecture across the system lifecycle. It also interfaces with SIEM and SOAR platforms to provide real-time telemetry, anomaly detection, and automated enforcement of security policies.

With modular APIs, FIPS 140-3 aligned encryption, and Kubernetes-driven orchestration, Andromeda supports SCAP validation for STIG compliance and enables GitOps-based deployments across development, test, and production environments.

Backed by Microsoft and Amazon, Andromeda is currently under consideration for submission to the NASA Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) program and the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) innovation pipeline.
