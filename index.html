Introducing Andromeda

Andromeda is a cloud agnostic cybersecurity and compliance automation platform built for the U.S. Department of Defense agencies. It simplifies the Risk Management Framework by automating control validation, evidence collection, and continuous monitoring across standards such as FISMA, NIST SP 800 53 Revision 5, FedRAMP, CMMC 2.0, and DFARS.

Supporting Microsoft Azure Government, AWS GovCloud, and future Impact Level 4 on premise workloads, Andromeda applies Infrastructure as Code, role based access control, and compliance as code to enforce security throughout the lifecycle. It integrates with SIEM and SOAR platforms to provide real time telemetry, anomaly detection, and automated policy enforcement.

With modular APIs, encryption aligned with FIPS 140 3 standards, and container orchestration using Kubernetes, Andromeda supports Zero Trust architecture and meets the expectations of Executive Order directives. It also enables SCAP validation for STIG compliance and integrates with GitOps workflows to support declarative deployments across development, test, and production environments.
